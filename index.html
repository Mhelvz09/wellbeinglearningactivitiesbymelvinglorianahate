Welcome to Your Learning Journey

This module is designed specifically for you - Bicolano ESL pre-service teachers.

I believe that learning English is not about being perfect. It's about growing as a person and as a future educator.

🤝 Bicolano Values We Honor

  • Malasakit - Compassion and genuine care
  • Pakikiramdam - Empathetic sensitivity
  • Bayanihan - Communal unity and teamwork
  • Pakikipagkapwa - Recognizing shared identity
  • Pagpapahalaga sa Karunungan - Valuing wisdom and knowledge

📚 The 7 Topics You'll Learn

Topic 1: Introduction to Communication

Learn the basics of communication - what it is, how it works, and why it matters.

Duration: Weeks 1-3 | Focus: Positive Emotion

Topic 2: Communication for Various Purposes

Discover how to communicate to inform, persuade, and connect with others.

Duration: Weeks 4-5 | Focus: Engagement

Topic 3: Communication Aids & Technology

Learn to use modern tools - presentations, videos, and digital platforms.

Duration: Weeks 6-7 | Focus: Engagement

Topic 4: Communication for Work

Master professional communication - interviews, letters, and presentations.

Duration: Weeks 8-10 | Focus: Meaning

Topic 5: Global & Multicultural Communication

Understand different cultures and how to communicate respectfully across borders.

Duration: Weeks 11-13 | Focus: Relationships

Topic 6: Language Varieties & Registers

Learn how to adjust your English for different situations and audiences.

Duration: Weeks 14-15 | Focus: Engagement + Meaning

Topic 7: Academic Communication

Develop skills for research, writing, and presenting academic work.

Duration: Weeks 16-18 | Focus: Accomplishment

🎯 14 Well-Being Activities

These activities are designed to help you grow while honoring your culture:

😊 Positive Emotion (Activities 1-3)

  • Activity 1: What Makes Me a Unique Communicator? - Write a reflection about yourself
  • Activity 2: Self-Compassionate Letter - Write a kind letter to yourself
  • Activity 3: Awesome Memories - Share and present positive experiences

🎯 Engagement (Activities 4, 5, 7, 12)

  • Activity 4: Survey - Create and conduct a happiness survey
  • Activity 5: Random Acts of Kindness - Perform kind acts for one week
  • Activity 7: Why We Communicate? - Create a video about communication
  • Activity 12: My Voice Across Contexts - Show how you speak differently in different situations

🤝 Relationships (Activities 6, 9)

  • Activity 6: The Power of Forgiveness - Create an art piece about forgiveness
  • Activity 9: Uniqueness of Filipinos & Irosanon - Group project comparing cultures

💡 Meaning (Activities 8, 10, 11)

  • Activity 8: Me as a Future Educator - Video about your teaching goals
  • Activity 10: How Should I Talk to You? - Learn different registers and styles
  • Activity 11: From Chat to Letter - Transform casual language to formal

🏆 Accomplishment (Activities 9-B, 13, 14)

  • Activity 9-B: Cover Letter - Write a professional cover letter for a job
  • Activity 13: Happiness Research - Survey and analyze student well-being
  • Activity 14: Havigraphy Campaign - Create an advocacy campaign about well-being

What is PERMA?

PERMA is a framework for well-being and happiness:

  • P - Positive Emotion: Feeling good and happy
  • E - Engagement: Being fully involved in what you do
  • R - Relationships: Having meaningful connections with others
  • M - Meaning: Doing things that matter and have purpose
  • A - Accomplishment: Achieving goals and growing
